Brand Principles

Call to Action — Contact Model
NO DIRECT ORDERING

Enigma Sports operates on a consultation model. Visitors are guided to reach out directly for samples, bulk orders, and custom quotes. All CTAs lead to email or WhatsApp — never a cart.

Rule: Never display individual product prices on the website. Direct all purchasing intent to email or WhatsApp for negotiation, sample orders, and bulk quotations.

CLOTHING MOCKUP STYLE

Clean studio presentation. Every garment photographed to the same standard.

Photography Standards
BACKGROUND

Pure white (#FFFFFF) or off-white (#F8F6F3). No gradients, no shadows on backdrop.

LIGHTING

Soft, even diffused lighting. No harsh shadows. Subtle shadow beneath garment for depth only.

SHOT ANGLES

Front flat-lay, front ghost mannequin, and back ghost mannequin for every SKU.

COMPOSITION

Centered. 10% padding on all sides minimum. No cropping of garment edges.

COLOR ACCURACY

Calibrated to sRGB. All colorways shot under identical conditions. No retouching of color.

RESOLUTION

Minimum 2000×2500px. Exported as sRGB JPEG 90% or WebP for web, PNG for print.

Branding on Product
Logo Placement

Left chest or center nape. Never centered on front unless the design calls for it explicitly.

Label Color

Woven label in black fabric, white text. Accent label variant uses a single red thread line only.

Tag Design

Hang tag in black matte cardstock. Logotype in white Bebas Neue. SKU in Courier. Red cord only.

Embroidery

Diamond logomark at 18px equivalent on physical scale. Never printed — always embroidered or woven.
