FAQ

COMMON
QUESTIONS

What is the minimum order quantity?
We offer flexible MOQs depending on the product. Sample orders of as few as 1–5 pieces are welcome. For bulk orders, MOQ typically starts at 25–50 units per style. Contact us to discuss your specific requirements.
Do you provide free design mockups?
Yes — always. Our design team creates detailed digital mockups of your product at no cost before any order is placed. You approve the design before we produce a single sample.
How long does sample production take?
Physical samples are typically ready within 7–10 business days after design approval. Rush samples can be arranged in some cases — ask us about lead times when you enquire.
Can I use my own branding on the products?
Absolutely. We specialise in private label manufacturing. Your logo, labels, hang tags, and packaging can all be customised. We produce the full brand experience, not just the garment.
Which countries do you ship to?
We ship globally. We work with trusted freight partners offering DHL, FedEx, and sea freight options depending on order size and your destination. We handle all export documentation.
Why are there no prices on the website?
Pricing depends on your product specifications, customisation requirements, and order quantity. We prefer to give you an accurate, personalised quote rather than a misleading general price. Reach out — it's quick and there's no obligation.
